Chapter 2: Entropy, Relative Entropy and Mutual Information

The entropy of a discrete random variable (drv) $X$ with probability mass function $p(x)$ is defined as:

$$H(X) = -\sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} p(x) \log p(x)$$
